How it works

From template to inbox in minutes

Get started quickly with built-in examples. No learning curve — open the app and you're ready to send.

1
Design your email template
Use the drag-and-drop editor to build a professional HTML email. Add your logo, images, buttons, and text blocks visually.
2
Import your contact list
Upload a CSV file. MailCraft auto-maps columns to merge fields like {{FirstName}}, {{Company}}, {{Email}} instantly.
3
Preview on every device
Check your email on mobile and desktop. Send a test to yourself to confirm everything looks perfect.
4
Send via Outlook
Connect your Outlook or Hotmail account and launch. Each recipient gets a uniquely personalized email from your address.
